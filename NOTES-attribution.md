# Attribution & licensing notes

Sources we draw on for the Cornell Claude Training site, and what each one requires for attribution. Used to populate `acknowledgments.html` (to be built).

> **Key consequence:** every source listed here is licensed CC BY-NC-SA 4.0. That license has a **ShareAlike** clause, which means the Cornell training materials (slide deck, module pages, exercise prompts) — to the extent they are derivative of these works — must also be released under **CC BY-NC-SA 4.0** with attribution. We should put a CC BY-NC-SA badge + line in the site footer and on any standalone deliverables.
