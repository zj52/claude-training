AI Fluency.

How to collaborate with Claude well — effectively, efficiently, ethically, and safely. We’ll review the framework, share the cohort’s word cloud from this week’s homework, and run an advanced example together.

The framework

4 dimensions · 4 Ds.

4 Dimensions

Effective

Get the result

Use Claude to produce work that actually meets your goal.

Efficient

Save the time

Pick tasks where Claude actually saves effort over doing it yourself.

Ethical

Mind the people

Use Claude in ways that respect the people whose work and data are involved.

Safe

Mind the data

Match the data classification to what the tool is approved for. No HIPAA. No export-controlled.

4 Ds

Delegation

What to hand over

Decide what goes to Claude vs. web search vs. spreadsheet vs. you.

Description

How to ask

Set the stage, give the task, specify the output. The Anthropic 3-part prompt.

Discernment

How to read it

Read the output critically. Spot-check facts, numbers, and citations.

Diligence

How to follow up

Iterate, revise, and verify before sharing.
